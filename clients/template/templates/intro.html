With Quickmit you can separate out and manage how your parents view and submit online enrollment (and re-enrollment) by whatever categories of student applications you would like to offer them in presenting your school programs, for example:

Only one grade level of education?
Our standard Quickmit application process immediately handles any education level focus you wish to differentiate for parents: Pre-K, Kindergarten, Elementary, Middle and High School.

More than one grade level category?
Quickmit can organize individual categories into any desired grouping of grade levels you wish to offer parents when they come to your website and use the Quickmit landing page you've customized.
